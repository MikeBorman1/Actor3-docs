# Income
Here you can find two graphs, one for GWP and the other for agency income.
Each graph has two data vectors 

- **New** - This is new insurance business 
- **Renewals** - Business that has been renewed.

## GWP Development

This graph shows the Gross Written Premium over the selected timeframe. Data is added to this graph only once a premium has been posted.

## Income Development

This Graph shows the Agency Income based on the commission variable that has been set on a posted premium.
